Texas Pulled Pork

Description

This pulled pork recipe is tender, juicy, and oh-so flavorful — and it's incredibly easy to make in your slow cooker!

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Pork
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Yellow mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Chicken broth
  • Brown sugar
  • Chili powder
  • Garlic
  • Dried thyme
  • Onion
  • Hamburger buns

Steps

  1. Pour oil into the slow cooker, then place the roast on top of the oil.
  2. Add the remainig ingredients to the Crock-Pot
  3. Cover and cook until the pork shreds easily
  4. Shred the pork and return it to the slow cooker to combine it with the juices
  5. If you're making sandwiches, serve the pulled pork on buttered buns
